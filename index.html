Intro

Welcome to my portfolio. As a software developer, this website is here to show some of the software I have worked on over the years, both at university and within my own solo projects. These involve a mix of front-end and back-end software development work.

As a developer, I strive to write clean and well tested code of a professional, high quality. I strive to give users of my software unique, positive and memorable experiences.
